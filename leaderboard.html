Season 1 Rules & Regulations

🗓 Season Duration

1 November – 30 November 2025

👤 Player Login

  • All players must log in using Google to participate.
  • Each Google account counts as one unique player profile.
  • Scores are automatically linked to your Google ID — no manual entry needed.
  • Logging out and switching accounts during a match is not allowed.

🏁 Gameplay Rules

  • Play any of the available games: Astro Jumper, Car Racer, Tic Tac Toe, Memory Match, Tower Defense, and more.
  • Only scores achieved between 1 Nov – 30 Nov will count toward the Season 1 leaderboard.
  • For multiplayer games, only verified results are accepted.
  • Any use of bots, scripts, or unfair methods leads to instant disqualification.

💰 Rewards

  • Top 1 player of each game wins ₹100 cash reward.
  • Winners will be announced on 1 December 2025 via the website and WhatsApp community.
  • Prizes will be sent through UPI or digital payment within 7 days.
  • In case of a tie, the player who reached the top score first will be declared the winner.

🌍 Leaderboard & Ranking

  • Rankings are updated in real time using Firebase.
  • Each player’s global rank and personal best appear on the main page.
  • The leaderboard resets after each season.

⚖ Fair Play Policy

  • One Google account = one player.
  • Any attempt to manipulate scores or exploit bugs will result in a ban.
  • The admin’s decision will be final and binding.

📢 Community & Support

  • Join the Falconix WhatsApp Community for updates, results, and announcements.
  • Feedback and game suggestions for Season 2 are always welcome!
